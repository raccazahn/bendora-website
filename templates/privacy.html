Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Bendora Technology ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at bendoratechnology.com and interact with our services. Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with its terms, please discontinue use of the website.

01 Information We Collect

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us. This includes:

  • Full Name — provided when you submit our contact form
  • Email Address — provided when you submit our contact form
  • Message Content — the subject and message you send us

We do not collect any payment information, passwords, government IDs, or sensitive personal data of any kind.

02 How We Collect Your Information

Information is collected exclusively through our contact form, which is powered by Formspree — a third-party form submission service. When you submit the contact form:

  • Your submission is processed and delivered to our email address via Formspree
  • Formspree may store your submission data on their servers
  • We receive your message directly in our Gmail inbox

You can review Formspree's own privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

03 How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiry or project request
  • To communicate with you about potential services or collaborations
  • To provide you with information you specifically requested

We will never use your information for unsolicited marketing, sell it to third parties, or share it with anyone outside of Bendora Technology without your explicit consent.

04 Cookies & Tracking

Our website does not use:

  • Tracking cookies or advertising cookies
  • Google Analytics or any third-party analytics tools
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Any form of behavioural tracking or profiling

We are committed to keeping your browsing experience private and free from unnecessary data collection.

05 Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Your contact form submissions are transmitted securely via Formspree using HTTPS encryption. Access to your submitted data is strictly limited to Bendora Technology personnel only. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

06 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access — you can request a copy of any data we hold about you
  • Right to Deletion — you can request that we delete your data at any time
  • Right to Correction — you can request that we correct any inaccurate information
  • Right to Object — you can object to how we use your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at bendoratechnology@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

07 Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and Twitter. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit through links on our website.

08 Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
